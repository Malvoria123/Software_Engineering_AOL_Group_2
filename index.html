CUSTOMER FAVORITES

Tonkutsu Ramen

A rich Japanese noodle soup with a creamy broth made from pork bones simmered for hours. It's served with thin noodles, tender chashu, green onions, wood ear mushrooms, and sometimes a soft-boiled egg.

Sushi

Fresh, high-quality fish and perfectly seasoned rice make our sushi an authentic Japanese experience.

Japanese Curry

A flavorful and mildly spicy curry served with tender chunks of meat and vegetables..
